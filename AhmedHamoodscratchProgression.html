Problem # Problem Solution
Problem #1 Moving with the hero The organization of move variables were hard to put together, So I used If statements to combine a group of instructions to one effect. Meaning I could make 4 clusters of groups in one forever loop.
Problem #2 Trouble gliding to random positions I had to make the antihero’s spawn in a random place on the right side of the screen then goto a random place on the left side of the screen. So I used pick random operators which allowed me to glide to a certain area or spot if I desired.
Problem #3 Being pushed back with the white rocket When you go near the white rocket I want you to reflect the exact way you came from, so if you were to hit the front of the rocket you would be pushed forward an equal amount. There was no operator to do that, So I had to get creative and use sensing to make you move an equal amount to the result hit.
Problem #4 Restarting The restart button would make everything stop but freeze for a while. The only way I could have fixed this button is by adding a delay so less items are frozen when you restart. Making the restarting almost perfect.

What would you do different?

For the next time I would start off by introducing a pair of instructions on the screen or making a control setting allowing you to see the controls and maybe switch from w,a,s, and d to the arrow keys. Also, I would like to increase the amount of levels instead of one. I could add maybe a random terrain generator, which would spawn random antiheros and give you a random but realistic star goal which would be displayed on the top right hand corner. As of the infinite mode, I would like more antiheros and power-ups giving you specific boosts for a certain amount of time.
